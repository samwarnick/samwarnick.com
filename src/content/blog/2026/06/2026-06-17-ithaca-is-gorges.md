I grew up near Ithaca, New York. One of the things Ithaca is known for is the slogan: Ithaca is Gorges. I love it. It's one of those IYKYK things that is fun to spot all over as you travel. I especially like the font. I believe it is [ITC Korinna](https://www.myfonts.com/collections/korinna-font-itc), a font made in the 1970s as a revival of the [Korinna typeface](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Korinna). The font feels very cozy and nostalgic to me.

Here's one of the many waterfalls near Ithaca, Taughannock Falls.
