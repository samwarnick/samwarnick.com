I needed a little extra motivation to write on the blog, so I proposed to a few friends a sort of challenge. Each week, one of us will choose the prompt for everyone and we have one week to blog about it. Pretty simple. So that's what we'll be doing in April! For the week of April 1–7, I picked "What's something cool I'm caring about or into recently?"[^1] Look out for that soon.
